# 🚖 Unveiling Driver Dynamics: Behavior & Job Satisfaction in Indian Ride-Hailing

## 📌 Project Overview

**Unveiling Driver Dynamics: Behavior & Job Satisfaction in Indian Ride-Hailing** is a statistical data analysis project focused on understanding driver behavior, earnings patterns, and job satisfaction in the ride-hailing industry.

This project examines how operational and financial factors such as **daily ride volume, earnings, working hours, incentives, and app performance** influence driver satisfaction levels. The insights generated from this analysis can help ride-hailing companies improve driver retention, optimize incentives, and enhance overall service efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze driver earnings and ride activity patterns  
- Understand factors affecting driver job satisfaction  
- Identify relationships between working hours and income  
- Study the impact of incentives on driver satisfaction  
- Evaluate the role of app performance in driver productivity  
- Provide actionable insights for improving driver retention  

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset consists of structured survey and operational data collected from ride-hailing drivers.

### Key Variables Included:

- Driver ID  
- Daily Ride Volume  
- Daily Earnings  
- Weekly Working Hours  
- Incentives Received  
- Fuel Cost  
- App Performance Rating  
- Customer Ratings  
- Job Satisfaction Score

## 📈 Key Insights

- Drivers completing more daily rides generally earn higher income.  
- Incentive availability plays a significant role in increasing job satisfaction.  
- Longer working hours do not always guarantee higher satisfaction.  
- App performance ratings strongly influence driver productivity and morale.  
- Fuel costs significantly affect overall profitability for drivers.
